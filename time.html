Frequently asked questions

Questions we normally ask about Luno

How do i open a luno account?

You can open a Luno account online through our website or by visiting any Luno branch. You’ll need a valid ID, proof of address, and a passport photograph.

What are the fees for Luno account?

Luno accounts come with low, transparent fees. Standard checking accounts have no monthly maintenance fee if you meet the minimum balance.

How can i reset my online password for luno?

Click on “Forgot Password” on the login page. Follow the prompts to verify your identity and set a new password. If you need help, contact our support team.

What is the interest rate on Luno savings account?

Our savings accounts offer competitive rates that vary depending on the account type. Current rates are displayed on our Savings & Investments page.

How do i apply for luno loan?

You can apply online through the Loans section of our website or in person at any branch. You’ll need to provide income details, employment information,
and supporting documents.

Does Luno offer mobile banking services?

Yes! With the Luno Mobile App, you can check balances, transfer funds, pay bills, and more anytime, anywhere.
